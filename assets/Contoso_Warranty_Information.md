## Standard Manufacturer Warranty

All products sold by Contoso Electronics include manufacturer warranties that cover defects in materials and workmanship. Warranty periods vary by product category and manufacturer.

---

## Warranty Coverage by Product Category

### Laptops and Computers

#### Dell Products (e.g., Dell XPS 15)
- **Standard Warranty:** 1 year parts and labor
- **Coverage Includes:**
  - Hardware defects and malfunctions
  - Display defects (dead pixels exceeding manufacturer specifications)
  - Keyboard and trackpad issues
  - Battery defects (coverage for batteries holding less than 50% of original capacity)
  - Power adapter failures
- **Exclusions:**
  - Accidental damage (drops, liquid spills)
  - Software issues and viruses
  - Cosmetic damage that doesn't affect functionality
  - Normal wear and tear (e.g., battery degradation under 50%)
- **Service Options:**
  - Mail-in service (5-7 business days)
  - Depot service available
  - On-site service available with extended warranty

#### Apple Products (MacBook, iMac)
- **Standard Warranty:** 1 year limited warranty
- **AppleCare+ Available:** Extended to 3 years with accidental damage coverage
- **Coverage:** Hardware repairs, battery service (if battery holds less than 80% capacity)

#### HP and Lenovo Products
- **Standard Warranty:** 1 year parts and labor
- **On-site service available** for business-class models
- **International warranty** valid in 100+ countries

## How to File a Warranty Claim

### Step 1: Determine Eligibility
- Check your purchase date and warranty period
- Review warranty terms for your specific product
- Ensure issue is covered under warranty terms

### Step 2: Contact Support
**For Manufacturer Warranty:**
- Contact manufacturer directly (contact info below)
- Have serial number and proof of purchase ready

**For Contoso Extended Protection:**
- Call 1-800-CONTOSO (1-800-266-8676)
- Email: warranty@contoso-electronics.com
- Online: www.contoso-electronics.com/warranty-claims

### Step 3: Provide Documentation
- Proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation)
- Product serial number or model number
- Description of the issue
- Photos or videos of the problem (if applicable)

### Step 4: Service Options
- **Mail-in Service:** Free prepaid shipping label provided
- **Depot Service:** Drop off at Contoso store
- **In-home Service:** Available for TVs over 42" and large appliances
- **Advanced Exchange:** Replacement shipped before return (deposit may be required)

### Step 5: Repair or Replacement
- **Repair:** Product fixed and returned within 7-10 business days
- **Replacement:** New or refurbished unit if repair not feasible
- **Refund:** If product discontinued or multiple repair failures

## Warranty Exclusions

### Not Covered Under Standard Warranty:
- **Accidental Damage:** Drops, impacts, liquid spills
- **Misuse:** Using product contrary to instructions
- **Environmental Damage:** Extreme temperatures, humidity, dust
- **Unauthorized Repairs:** Third-party modifications or repairs
- **Cosmetic Damage:** Scratches, dents that don't affect function
- **Lost or Stolen Items:** Requires theft protection plan
- **Software Issues:** Viruses, malware, OS corruption
- **Consumables:** Batteries (beyond specified capacity), ink, toner

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What if I don't have my receipt?**  
A: We can look up your purchase in our system using your email, phone number, or credit card. For warranty claims, the product serial number can also help verify purchase date.

**Q: Can I purchase an extended warranty after my purchase?**  
A: Yes, extended protection plans can be purchased within 30 days of your original purchase date.

**Q: What's the difference between refurbished and new replacements?**  
A: Refurbished units are thoroughly tested and certified. They carry the same warranty as new units. You can request a new replacement for an additional fee.

**Q: How long do repairs usually take?**  
A: Most repairs are completed within 7-10 business days. Complex repairs may take up to 15 business days. We'll provide status updates throughout the process.

**Q: What if my product fails multiple times?**  
A: If your product requires repair more than 3 times for the same issue, or cannot be repaired, we'll offer a replacement or refund at your choice.

**Q: Does warranty cover international travel?**  
A: Most manufacturer warranties are valid internationally. Contoso Extended Protection Plans are valid in the US, Canada, and Mexico. Contact us for international coverage details.

**Q: What happens if my product is discontinued?**  
A: You'll receive a comparable replacement of equal or greater value, or a refund/credit for the original purchase price.

**Q: Can I transfer my warranty if I sell my product?**  
A: Manufacturer warranties typically transfer with the product. Contoso Extended Protection Plans are non-transferable unless specifically noted.

## Battery Coverage Details

### Laptops and Tablets
- Covered if battery capacity drops below **50%** within warranty period
- Testing required to verify capacity
- One battery replacement per warranty period

### Smartphones
- Covered if battery health falls below **80%** (Apple)
- Other manufacturers typically cover below **50%** capacity

### Headphones and Wireless Devices
- Covered if battery won't hold charge or depletes rapidly
- Must demonstrate significant degradation from original specifications

### Note on Battery Wear
Batteries naturally degrade over time. Coverage applies only when degradation exceeds normal wear patterns for product age.
