# Contoso Electronics Return Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Last Updated:** January 15, 2026

## Overview

At Contoso Electronics, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your product, we offer a flexible return policy to ensure your satisfaction.

---

## Return Windows

### Standard Electronics
- **30-day return window** from date of delivery
- Products must be in original condition with all accessories
- Original packaging required for full refund

### Headphones and Audio Equipment
- **30-day return window** for unopened items
- **14-day return window** for opened items (hygiene reasons)
- Must include all original accessories, cables, and carrying cases

### Large Appliances (TVs, Refrigerators, Washing Machines)
- **45-day return window**
- Free return shipping for items over 50 lbs
- Professional removal service available for $50

### Computers and Laptops
- **30-day return window**
- Software licenses are non-refundable once activated
- Custom-configured systems may be subject to 15% restocking fee

### Opened Software and Digital Products
- **Non-refundable** once license key is activated
- Defective software can be exchanged within 30 days

---

## Return Conditions

### Eligible for Full Refund
- Product is in original, unused condition
- All accessories, manuals, and packaging included
- No signs of wear or damage
- Returned within applicable return window

### Eligible for Partial Refund (85% of purchase price)
- Product shows minor signs of use
- Missing non-essential accessories (e.g., extra cables)
- Original packaging damaged but product intact

### Not Eligible for Return
- Products damaged due to misuse or negligence
- Items without original proof of purchase
- Products returned after the return window expires
- Health and personal care items that have been opened
- Custom-ordered or personalized products

## How to Initiate a Return

### Online Returns (Recommended)
1. Log into your Contoso account at www.contoso-electronics.com
2. Navigate to "Order History"
3. Select the order and click "Return Items"
4. Choose your reason for return
5. Print the prepaid shipping label
6. Package the item securely and drop off at any authorized carrier location

### In-Store Returns
1. Bring the product with original packaging and accessories
2. Provide proof of purchase (order confirmation email or receipt)
3. Visit any Contoso Electronics retail location
4. Our associate will process your return immediately

### Phone Returns
1. Call our customer service at 1-800-CONTOSO (1-800-266-8676)
2. Have your order number ready
3. Agent will email you a prepaid return shipping label
4. Package and ship within 5 business days

## Refund Processing

### Timeframes
- **Credit Card:** 5-7 business days after we receive your return
- **Debit Card:** 7-10 business days after we receive your return
- **PayPal:** 3-5 business days after we receive your return
- **Store Credit:** Immediate upon return receipt

### Refund Methods
- Refunds issued to original payment method
- Option to receive store credit for 10% bonus value
- Gift card purchases refunded as store credit only

### Shipping Costs
- Free return shipping on defective items
- Free return shipping on orders over $500
- $15 return shipping fee for standard returns under $500
- Customers responsible for return shipping costs on "change of mind" returns under $100

## Exchanges

### Product Exchanges
- **Free exchanges** for different size, color, or model
- Price difference applies for upgraded models
- Downgrades refund the difference to original payment method
- Exchange shipping is free in both directions

### Defective Product Exchanges
- Immediate replacement shipped at no cost
- Keep defective item until replacement arrives
- Return defective item using prepaid label
- No restocking or shipping fees

## Special Circumstances

### Holiday Returns (November 1 - December 31)

### Business/B2B Purchases
- Custom return policies available for business accounts

### Open-Box and Clearance Items

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I return an opened product?**  
A: Yes, most products can be returned opened within the return window. Some hygiene-related items (headphones, earbuds) have shorter return windows once opened.

**Q: What if I lost my receipt?**  
A: If you purchased online, we can look up your order by email or order number. For in-store purchases without a receipt, we can offer store credit at the current selling price.

**Q: Can I return a gift?**  
A: Yes, gift recipients can return items for store credit. The original purchaser can also return gifts for a refund to their original payment method.

**Q: What if my product arrived damaged?**  
A: Contact us immediately at 1-800-CONTOSO. We'll ship a replacement at no cost and provide a prepaid label for the damaged item.

**Q: Do you offer price adjustments?**  
A: Yes! If an item you purchased goes on sale within 14 days, we'll refund the difference. Contact customer service with your order number.
